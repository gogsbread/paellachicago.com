PAELLA has been a tradition in my family for many years. Cooking paella is a passion that I inherited from my grandmothers who were natives of VALENCIA and BURGOS, two wonderful states in Spain.


Growing up in Argentina, I honed my talent in the art of making delicious paellas and here I bring thee an authentic spanish delicacy.


Paella is a dish base on rice, vegetables, seafood, fish, chicken, pork and chorizos but the key ingredient for the fantastic flavor is the Spanish saffron and the unique spice blend.


There are different types of paellas, each characterising its city of origin. Paellas are more seafood based along the coast, and more meat based in the country. Paellas can also be prepared in a vegetarian style.
